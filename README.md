# 📖 About Aureon

Aureon is a native iOS investing platform currently in development that helps investors better understand financial markets through AI-driven analysis, portfolio tracking, ETF flow monitoring, and real-time market intelligence.

Rather than switching between multiple apps, Aureon brings market data, AI insights, and portfolio intelligence together into one seamless experience.

**My goal is simple:**

Help investors make better decisions by turning complex market data into clear, actionable insights.

---

# 💡 Why Aureon?

As an investor, I found myself constantly switching between multiple apps to track my portfolio, monitor ETF activity, research market trends, and interpret complex financial data.

I started building Aureon to create the investing assistant I always wished existed—one intelligent platform that helps investors make faster, smarter, and more confident decisions.
