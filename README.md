# 🚀 Aureon

> An AI-powered investing assistant that combines portfolio management, market intelligence, ETF flows, and real-time insights into one seamless experience.

Aureon is a native iOS application currently in development that helps investors understand the market through AI-driven analysis, portfolio tracking, ETF flow monitoring, and real-time cryptocurrency data.

My goal is simple:

> Help investors make better decisions by turning complex market data into clear, actionable insights.

---

# 📱 Why Aureon?

As an investor, I found myself constantly switching between multiple apps to track my portfolio, monitor ETF activity, research market trends, and interpret complex data.

I started building Aureon to bring everything together into a single, intelligent investing assistant that simplifies research and helps investors make more informed decisions.

---
